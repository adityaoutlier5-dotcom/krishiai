# Regional Practices — State-wise Snapshots

## Punjab & Haryana
- **Mainstay**: wheat–paddy rotation. Heavy groundwater depletion (Punjab losing ~1m/year in south).
- **Push**: crop diversification — maize, cotton, pulses, oilseeds, basmati instead of coarse paddy.
- **Residue**: DON'T burn — use **Happy Seeder** (80% subsidy), bio-decomposer (Pusa), balers.
- **Soil**: alkaline in SW Punjab (Bathinda, Mansa) — gypsum + organic amendments.
- **Varieties**: wheat HD-3226, PBW-725; paddy PR-126 (short duration, saves water).
- **Schemes**: In-situ crop residue management (₹62/acre incentive recently); Mera Pani Meri Virasat (Haryana — cash for NOT planting paddy).

## Western UP (sugarcane belt) + Eastern UP (rice–wheat)
- **West UP**: sugarcane + wheat. Co-0238 red rot issues — KVK to recommend replacement variety.
- **East UP / Bihar**: flood-prone — Sahbhagi dhan (drought), Swarna Sub-1 (flood).
- **Dairy**: massive sector — link via Amul / local dairies; KCC-AHDF.

## Bihar & Jharkhand
- **Crops**: paddy, maize (rabi — big in Kosi belt), wheat, pulses, vegetables, litchi (Muzaffarpur).
- **Flood/drought cyclic** — crop insurance essential.
- **Rice-fallow pulse**: huge opportunity — intercrop masoor/chana after paddy.

## West Bengal
- **Paddy + jute + potato** rotations dominate.
- **Aman, Aus, Boro** paddy — three cycles possible.
- **Pest watch**: stem borer, BPH.

## Odisha
- **Paddy + pulses** coastal; **oilseeds + pulses** western districts.
- **Cyclones frequent** — PMFBY + early-maturing varieties (Naveen, Lalat).
- **KALIA scheme**: ₹10,000/year support.

## Assam & NE states
- **Crops**: paddy (sali — main), mustard, jute, tea, horticulture (pineapple, orange, banana, ginger, turmeric).
- **Acidic soil**: add lime 200–500 kg/acre.
- **Horticulture focus** under MIDH + Mission Organic Value Chain (MOVCDNER).

## Madhya Pradesh
- **Soybean + wheat** is the mainstay (Malwa plateau).
- **Gram, mustard, paddy** also significant.
- **Orange (Chhindwara), banana, spices** growing.
- **Soil**: black cotton in some districts; red-yellow in others.
- **MP Govt**: Bhavantar Bhugtan Yojana (price deficit compensation on select crops).

## Chhattisgarh
- **Paddy bowl** — state procures at MSP + bonus (₹2500–2800/q in past, varies).
- **Rajiv Gandhi Kisan Nyay Yojana (RGKNY)**: input support per acre.

## Maharashtra
- **Vidarbha (east)**: cotton + soybean + tur. Chronic distress — drip + Bt + crop insurance.
- **Marathwada**: drought, pulses, cotton. Focus: jalyukt shivar (water conservation), drip.
- **W. Maharashtra**: sugarcane + grape + pomegranate. Drip mandatory.
- **Konkan**: mango (Alphonso), cashew, coconut, paddy.
- **Dairy belt**: Pune, Ahmednagar, Nashik — Amul / Chitale.

## Gujarat
- **Cotton (No.1 state), groundnut, castor, cumin, tobacco**.
- **Amul cooperative dairy model** — world-famous.
- **Saurashtra + Kutch**: arid — drip mandatory; crop: groundnut, cumin, cotton.
- **Horticulture**: mango (Kesar), banana (Anand), potato (Deesa).

## Rajasthan
- **Bajra, mustard, guar, chana, cumin** dominate.
- **Tharparkar camel / Gir cattle** traditions.
- **Irrigation**: Indira Gandhi Canal (NW); rest is rainfed — drought-tolerant crops + micro-irrigation.
- **Mustard hub** — Alwar, Bharatpur.

## Karnataka
- **Ragi, jowar, arecanut, coffee, coconut, sugarcane** (N Karnataka).
- **Dryland tracts**: focus millets, pulses; **Krishi Bhagya** farm-pond scheme (90% subsidy for SC/ST/small) — game-changer.
- **Malnad**: arecanut, pepper, coffee.
- **Coastal**: paddy, coconut, cashew, fisheries.
- **Dairy**: Nandini brand (KMF).
- **Byadgi chilli, Nanjangud banana, Coorg mandarin** — GI crops.

## Tamil Nadu
- **Paddy** (Cauvery delta), **sugarcane**, **cotton** (southern).
- **Banana** (Tiruchirappalli), **coconut, turmeric, chilli, cardamom** (hills).
- **SRI method** of paddy: ~20% higher yield, 40% less water.
- **Free power to agri pumps** — but encourage efficient use.
- **Uzhavan App**: gov't farmer app for schemes + weather + prices.

## Andhra Pradesh & Telangana
- **AP**: paddy (Krishna/Godavari), chilli (Guntur — Asia's largest market), cotton, groundnut, tobacco, mango (Banganapalli), sugarcane.
- **Telangana**: paddy (Rythu Bandhu input support ₹10k/acre — check current), cotton, maize, chilli.
- **Horticulture rising**: mango, sweet orange, watermelon, papaya.
- **ZBNF** large state-level programme in AP.

## Kerala
- **Perennials dominate**: coconut, rubber, pepper, cardamom, tea, coffee.
- **Paddy** in Kuttanad (below sea level — special management).
- **Pest focus**: rhinoceros beetle, red palm weevil (coconut); root wilt (coconut — incurable).
- **KAU varieties** + tissue-culture planting material essential.
- **Organic + high-value spices** export-ready.

## Goa
- **Coconut, cashew, mango, paddy, kokum, feni crops**.
- **Organic farming push**.

## Hill states (Himachal, Uttarakhand, J&K)
- **Apple** (HP — Kullu, Shimla; J&K — Kashmir).
- **Off-season vegetables** (tomato, capsicum, peas — fetch premium in plains).
- **Kiwi, peach, apricot, walnut, saffron (Pampore)**.
- **Terracing + contour bunds** essential to prevent erosion.
- **Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH)** subsidies heavy.

## Fishery states (coastal Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka, Gujarat, Maharashtra, Odisha)
- **Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY)**: huge scheme for fisheries — cold chain, boats, ponds.
- **KCC extended to fisheries** (AHDF-KCC).
