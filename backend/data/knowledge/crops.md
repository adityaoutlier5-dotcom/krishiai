# Major Indian Crops — Package of Practices

## Paddy (Rice) — Kharif
- **Sowing**: June–July (onset of monsoon). Transplant 21–25 day seedlings.
- **Varieties**: Swarna Sub-1 (flood-tolerant), MTU-1010, Pusa Basmati-1121, Sahbhagi (drought).
- **Spacing**: 20 × 15 cm (normal), 25 × 25 cm (SRI method — 20% more yield with less water).
- **Seed rate**: 20–25 kg/acre for transplant; 40 kg/acre for direct-seeded rice (DSR).
- **Fertilizer per acre**: 25 kg DAP + 25 kg MOP basal; 50 kg urea at tillering (20–25 DAS); 25 kg urea at panicle initiation (45–50 DAS).
- **Water**: 1200–1500 mm total; alternate wetting-drying (AWD) saves ~30% water without yield loss.
- **Major pests**: stem borer, brown plant hopper, gall midge. IPM: pheromone traps, resistant varieties, neem oil 5 ml/L.
- **Diseases**: blast, bacterial leaf blight, sheath blight. Seed treatment with Trichoderma 4 g/kg; tricyclazole 75 WP @ 0.6 g/L for blast outbreak.
- **Harvest**: Sept–Oct, when 80% panicles are straw-coloured.
- **Yield**: 20–25 q/acre good practice; up to 30+ q with SRI.

## Wheat — Rabi
- **Sowing**: mid-Oct to mid-Nov (late sowing loses ~1% yield per day after Nov 15).
- **Varieties**: HD-3226 (rust-resistant), DBW-187, PBW-725, HD-2967.
- **Seed rate**: 40 kg/acre (timely); 50 kg/acre (late).
- **Fertilizer per acre**: 50 kg DAP + 25 kg MOP basal at sowing; 50 kg urea at first irrigation (CRI, ~21 DAS); 25 kg urea at second irrigation (tillering, ~40 DAS).
- **Irrigation**: 4–6 times — CRI, tillering, jointing, flowering, milk, dough stages.
- **Pests**: aphid, armyworm. IPM: ladybird beetles (natural predator), neem 5 ml/L; if severe, imidacloprid 17.8 SL @ 0.3 ml/L.
- **Diseases**: yellow rust, brown rust, loose smut. Sow resistant varieties; propiconazole 25 EC @ 1 ml/L if rust spotted.
- **Harvest**: March–April, when grain is hard and moisture ~20%.
- **Yield**: 18–22 q/acre good practice.

## Cotton — Kharif
- **Sowing**: April–May (irrigated), June (rainfed).
- **Varieties**: Bt cotton (Bollgard II), desi cotton (Suraj, NH-615) for organic/resistant areas.
- **Spacing**: 90 × 60 cm (Bt), 60 × 30 cm (desi).
- **Seed rate**: 1.5–2 kg/acre for Bt; 4 kg/acre for desi.
- **Fertilizer per acre**: 40 kg DAP + 25 kg MOP basal; 50 kg urea split — squaring (30 DAS), flowering (60 DAS).
- **Pests (high risk)**: pink bollworm, whitefly, thrips, jassid. IPM: pheromone traps 8/acre, refugia plantings, neem oil; emamectin benzoate 5 SG @ 0.4 g/L as last resort.
- **Diseases**: leaf curl virus (vector: whitefly), root rot. Control whitefly = control virus.
- **Harvest**: Oct–Jan, multiple pickings (3–5).
- **Yield**: 10–15 q/acre (Bt); 6–8 q (desi).
- **Special**: Maharashtra Vidarbha — drip irrigation + mulching saves 40% water.

## Sugarcane — Long-duration (12 months)
- **Planting**: Feb–March (spring), Oct–Nov (autumn, higher yield).
- **Varieties**: Co-0238, CoM-0265, Co-86032.
- **Seed rate**: 35–40 q/acre of 3-budded setts.
- **Fertilizer per acre**: 125 kg urea + 75 kg DAP + 50 kg MOP, split across 3 doses (planting, 45 DAS, 90 DAS).
- **Irrigation**: 8–10 in subtropical; 25+ in tropical (Maharashtra/Karnataka).
- **Pests**: early shoot borer, top borer, pyrilla. IPM: Trichogramma release 50k/acre × 6 times.
- **Diseases**: red rot, smut. Use resistant varieties; dip setts in 0.1% Carbendazim.
- **Harvest**: 10–12 months; test sucrose at 18%+ Brix.
- **Yield**: 350–450 q/acre; 500+ with drip + organic practices.

## Maize — Kharif / Rabi / Zaid (3 seasons)
- **Sowing**: Kharif Jun–Jul; Rabi Oct–Nov (irrigated); Zaid Feb–March.
- **Varieties**: DHM-117, Pioneer hybrids, Shaktiman series, QPM (quality protein) for feed.
- **Seed rate**: 8 kg/acre.
- **Spacing**: 60 × 20 cm.
- **Fertilizer per acre**: 30 kg DAP + 20 kg MOP basal; 50 kg urea split at knee-high and tasseling.
- **Pests**: Fall armyworm (major post-2018 threat), stem borer. Hand-pick egg masses; Bt spray at whorl; chlorantraniliprole 18.5 SC @ 0.4 ml/L if heavy.
- **Harvest**: 90–110 days.
- **Yield**: 20–30 q/acre hybrid.

## Tur (Arhar / Pigeon pea) — Kharif
- **Sowing**: June–July.
- **Varieties**: Pusa-9, ICPH-2740 (hybrid), BDN-711.
- **Seed rate**: 6–8 kg/acre (short-duration); 4 kg/acre (long-duration).
- **Fertilizer per acre**: 20 kg DAP + 10 kg MOP basal; no urea (pulse — fixes N).
- **Pests**: Helicoverpa (pod borer), pod fly. NPV 500 LE + neem; HaNPV 250 LE/acre.
- **Harvest**: Dec–Feb.
- **Yield**: 8–12 q/acre.
- **Benefit**: fixes 60–80 kg N/ha — excellent rotation crop.

## Chana (Gram / Chickpea) — Rabi
- **Sowing**: Oct–Nov.
- **Varieties**: JG-11, JAKI-9218, Pusa-256.
- **Seed rate**: 30 kg/acre.
- **Fertilizer per acre**: 20 kg DAP basal; Rhizobium + PSB seed inoculation (essential for pulses).
- **Pests**: pod borer (Helicoverpa). NPV + pheromone traps; emamectin benzoate if severe.
- **Diseases**: wilt, ascochyta blight. Seed treatment with Trichoderma 4 g/kg.
- **Harvest**: Feb–March.
- **Yield**: 8–12 q/acre.

## Mustard (Sarson) — Rabi
- **Sowing**: Oct (timely — by Oct 25).
- **Varieties**: Pusa Bold, RH-749, Giriraj.
- **Seed rate**: 2 kg/acre.
- **Fertilizer per acre**: 40 kg DAP + 20 kg MOP basal; 40 kg urea at flowering.
- **Pests**: aphid (main threat), sawfly. Dimethoate 30 EC @ 1 ml/L or neem oil 5 ml/L.
- **Harvest**: Feb–March.
- **Yield**: 8–10 q/acre.

## Groundnut — Kharif (main) / Rabi in South
- **Sowing**: June–July (Kharif); Nov–Dec (Rabi, South India).
- **Varieties**: TMV-2, GJG-9, ICGV-91114 (drought).
- **Seed rate**: 50–60 kg/acre (pods).
- **Fertilizer per acre**: 20 kg DAP + 20 kg MOP + gypsum 100 kg at flowering (crucial — provides Ca for pod filling).
- **Pests**: leaf miner, thrips, white grub. IPM; chlorpyriphos 20 EC if white grub.
- **Diseases**: tikka (leaf spot), rust, collar rot. Mancozeb 75 WP @ 2.5 g/L.
- **Harvest**: 110–120 days.
- **Yield**: 12–18 q/acre pods.

## Soybean — Kharif
- **Sowing**: June (MP, Maharashtra, Rajasthan).
- **Varieties**: JS-335, JS-9560, JS-2034.
- **Seed rate**: 30 kg/acre.
- **Fertilizer**: 20 kg DAP basal; Rhizobium inoculation.
- **Pests**: girdle beetle, semilooper. Thiamethoxam 25 WG @ 0.2 g/L.
- **Diseases**: yellow mosaic virus (whitefly-borne), rust.
- **Harvest**: Sep–Oct, 95–110 days.
- **Yield**: 10–15 q/acre.

## Bajra (Pearl Millet) — Kharif
- **Sowing**: Jun–Jul (dryland — excellent drought tolerance).
- **Varieties**: HHB-67, HHB-226, GHB-538.
- **Seed rate**: 1.5 kg/acre.
- **Fertilizer**: 20 kg DAP + 10 kg MOP; 20 kg urea at knee-high.
- **Harvest**: Sept–Oct.
- **Yield**: 8–12 q/acre rainfed.
- **Note**: Rajasthan/Haryana/Gujarat mainstay. Part of 2023 International Year of Millets push.

## Ragi (Finger Millet) — Kharif (Karnataka focus)
- **Sowing**: June–July.
- **Varieties**: GPU-28, ML-365, Indaf-9.
- **Seed rate**: 4 kg/acre (transplant), 5 kg/acre (direct).
- **Fertilizer**: 20 kg DAP + 10 kg MOP basal; 20 kg urea top-dress.
- **Harvest**: 110–125 days.
- **Yield**: 10–14 q/acre.
- **Note**: Karnataka's staple dryland crop; ICDS / midday-meal demand strong.

## Jowar (Sorghum) — Kharif / Rabi
- **Sowing**: June–July (Kharif); Sep–Oct (Rabi, Maharashtra/Karnataka).
- **Varieties**: CSH-14, CSV-15, Maldandi (Rabi).
- **Seed rate**: 3–4 kg/acre.
- **Pests**: shoot fly, stem borer. Seed treatment with imidacloprid 70 WS @ 7 g/kg.
- **Harvest**: Sep–Nov.
- **Yield**: 10–14 q/acre.

## Tomato — 3 seasons in India
- **Sowing**: Jun–Jul, Oct–Nov, Jan–Feb (depending on climate).
- **Varieties**: Arka Rakshak, Pusa Ruby, Abhinav (hybrid).
- **Spacing**: 60 × 45 cm.
- **Fertilizer per acre**: 8–10 tons FYM basal; 40 kg DAP + 25 kg MOP basal; 50 kg urea in 2–3 splits.
- **Pests**: fruit borer (Helicoverpa), leaf miner, whitefly. Pheromone traps + emamectin benzoate 5 SG @ 0.4 g/L.
- **Diseases**: early blight, late blight, leaf curl virus, bacterial wilt. Mancozeb 75 WP @ 2.5 g/L; metalaxyl rotation.
- **Harvest**: 90–120 days, multiple pickings over 2 months.
- **Yield**: 150–250 q/acre (hybrid, drip + stake).

## Onion — Kharif (late Jun) / Rabi (Nov)
- **Sowing**: May–Jun (Kharif); Oct–Nov (Rabi — higher yield).
- **Varieties**: Agri Found Dark Red, Nashik Red, Bhima Super.
- **Seed rate**: 4 kg/acre (transplant); 8 kg (direct).
- **Fertilizer**: 40 kg DAP + 25 kg MOP basal; 50 kg urea + 50 kg SOP (sulphur — vital for pungency) in 2 splits.
- **Pests**: thrips (main), onion fly. Lambda-cyhalothrin 5 EC @ 0.5 ml/L.
- **Diseases**: purple blotch, basal rot. Mancozeb 75 WP @ 2.5 g/L.
- **Storage**: cure in shade 10 days; proper ventilated storage keeps 5–6 months.
- **Yield**: 120–150 q/acre.

## Potato — Rabi (mainly Oct–Feb)
- **Planting**: Oct–Nov (UP, Bihar, Punjab); Sep (hills).
- **Varieties**: Kufri Jyoti, Kufri Pukhraj, Kufri Bahar.
- **Seed rate**: 8–10 q/acre (tubers 30–50 g).
- **Spacing**: 60 × 20 cm on ridges.
- **Fertilizer**: 50 kg DAP + 30 kg MOP basal; 60 kg urea in 2 splits.
- **Pests**: tuber moth, cutworm.
- **Diseases**: late blight (DANGEROUS — can wipe out crop in days). Prophylactic mancozeb; on wet foggy days, spray immediately. Certified disease-free seed essential.
- **Harvest**: 90–110 days.
- **Yield**: 100–150 q/acre.

## Chilli — Kharif (main)
- **Sowing**: May–Jun for transplant.
- **Varieties**: Byadgi (Karnataka — famous for colour), Teja, Pusa Jwala.
- **Spacing**: 75 × 45 cm.
- **Fertilizer**: 40 kg DAP + 20 kg MOP basal; 50 kg urea in 3 splits.
- **Pests**: thrips, mites, fruit borer. Spinosad 45 SC @ 0.3 ml/L for thrips.
- **Diseases**: leaf curl (thrips + whitefly vectors), anthracnose. Mancozeb 75 WP + neem 5 ml/L.
- **Harvest**: green — 90 days; red — 140 days.
- **Yield**: dry — 8–12 q/acre; green — 60–80 q/acre.

## Banana — Perennial
- **Planting**: June–August (tissue culture plants preferred).
- **Varieties**: G-9 (Grand Naine), Robusta, Nendran (Kerala), Poovan.
- **Spacing**: 1.8 × 1.8 m.
- **Fertilizer per plant**: 200 g urea + 200 g SSP + 300 g MOP, in 4 splits over 8 months.
- **Pests**: pseudostem weevil, nematode.
- **Diseases**: Panama wilt, Sigatoka leaf spot. Use tissue-culture planting material (certified).
- **Harvest**: 12–14 months.
- **Yield**: 25–30 t/acre.

## Mango — Perennial (long gestation)
- **Planting**: June–August, 10 × 10 m (or 5 × 5 m high-density).
- **Varieties**: Alphonso (Maharashtra), Kesar (Gujarat), Dasheri (UP), Banganapalli (AP), Langra, Totapuri.
- **First crop**: 4–5 years (grafted).
- **Manuring per tree (adult)**: 50 kg FYM + 1 kg urea + 500 g SSP + 1 kg MOP annually.
- **Pests**: mango hopper, fruit fly, mealy bug. Imidacloprid 17.8 SL @ 0.3 ml/L at flowering.
- **Diseases**: anthracnose, powdery mildew. Copper oxychloride at panicle stage.
- **Yield**: 100–200 kg/tree (adult), 10–15 years peak.

## Coconut — Perennial (Kerala, TN, Karnataka, AP)
- **Planting**: June–Sept (monsoon), 7.5 × 7.5 m.
- **Varieties**: West Coast Tall, Chowghat Orange Dwarf, hybrids (DxT, TxD).
- **First yield**: 6–7 years (dwarf earlier).
- **Manuring per palm (adult)**: 500 g urea + 350 g SSP + 1.2 kg MOP + organic manure, annually split in 2.
- **Pests**: rhinoceros beetle, red palm weevil. Hygiene + pheromone traps.
- **Diseases**: root wilt (Kerala — major), bud rot. Certified planting material essential.
- **Yield**: 80–100 nuts/palm/year good orchard.
