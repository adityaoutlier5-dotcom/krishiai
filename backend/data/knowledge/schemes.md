# Government Schemes for Indian Farmers

All amounts and eligibility criteria as of the last stable update — advise farmers to CONFIRM latest details at nearest CSC / Krishi Vigyan Kendra / agri office, as policies change.

## PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi)
- **What**: ₹6,000/year direct income support to landholding farmer families.
- **How paid**: 3 equal instalments of ₹2,000, direct to Aadhaar-linked bank account every 4 months.
- **Who**: all landholding farmer families (excluding high-income / institutional / govt-employee households).
- **Apply**: pmkisan.gov.in, or nearest Common Service Centre (CSC), or agri officer at tehsil/block.
- **Documents**: Aadhaar, land records (khasra/khatauni), bank account (Aadhaar-seeded).
- **e-KYC mandatory**: do on portal or via bank/CSC — otherwise instalment is held.

## PMFBY (Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana)
- **What**: crop insurance against natural calamities, pests, diseases (yield + weather losses).
- **Premium farmer pays**:
  - Kharif food/oilseed: **2%** of sum insured
  - Rabi food/oilseed: **1.5%**
  - Horticulture/commercial: **5%**
  - Balance subsidised by Centre + State.
- **Apply**: before cut-off date (usually July for Kharif, December for Rabi). Bank / CSC / pmfby.gov.in.
- **Claim**: auto-triggered if CCE (crop cutting experiment) yield is below threshold, or via crop loss form for individual claims.

## KCC (Kisan Credit Card)
- **What**: short-term crop loan + input finance.
- **Limit**: up to **₹3 lakh** at **7% interest**. With prompt repayment, **3% rebate** — effective **4%**.
- **Extended to**: dairy, fisheries, animal husbandry (AHDF-KCC).
- **Collateral-free limit**: ₹1.6 lakh (recently raised from ₹1 lakh).
- **Apply**: any commercial bank / RRB / cooperative bank; simplified one-page form.

## Soil Health Card (SHC)
- **What**: FREE soil test once every 2–3 years — reports N, P, K, pH, organic carbon, S, Zn, Fe, Cu, Mn, B.
- **Apply**: agri officer at block office / soilhealth.dac.gov.in.
- **Use**: matches exact fertilizer dose to your soil — saves money and prevents overdose.

## PMKSY (Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana) — Per Drop More Crop
- **What**: subsidy on micro-irrigation (drip, sprinkler).
- **Subsidy**: **55%** for small & marginal farmers; **45%** for other farmers (Centre + State share; state may add top-up).
- **Apply**: state horticulture / agri engineering department; pmksy.gov.in.

## PM-KUSUM (Kisan Urja Suraksha Evam Utthaan Mahabhiyan)
- **What**: solar pumps + solarisation of farm lands.
- **Component A**: ground-mounted solar plants (0.5 to 2 MW) on fallow land — sell electricity to DISCOMs.
- **Component B**: standalone solar pumps — up to **60% subsidy** (Centre 30% + State 30%), farmer pays ~40% or via loan.
- **Component C**: solarisation of existing grid-connected pumps — farmer can sell surplus power.
- **Apply**: state renewable energy development agency (SNA), or MNRE portal.

## e-NAM (National Agriculture Market)
- **What**: online unified mandi — pan-India trading platform.
- **Benefit**: discover prices across mandis, higher bargaining, transparent weighment.
- **Register**: enam.gov.in (with nearest APMC mandi); Aadhaar + bank + land docs.

## Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF)
- **What**: ₹1 lakh crore fund for post-harvest infra — cold storage, warehouses, grading, pack-houses, custom hiring centres.
- **Benefit**: 3% interest subvention + credit guarantee on loans up to ₹2 crore.
- **Eligible**: FPOs, PACS, farmers, agri-entrepreneurs.
- **Apply**: via any scheduled bank or agriinfra.dac.gov.in.

## PM Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PMFME)
- **What**: 35% subsidy up to ₹10 lakh for micro food-processing units.
- **Eligible**: individual farmers, FPOs, SHGs, cooperatives.
- **Apply**: pmfme.mofpi.gov.in; state nodal agency.

## National Mission on Oilseeds & Oil Palm (NMEO-OP)
- **What**: subsidies for oil palm planting + assured MSP-like price mechanism.
- **Target states**: NE, Andhra, Telangana, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Odisha.

## PM-PRANAM
- **What**: incentive for states promoting alternative/organic/bio-fertilisers to reduce chemical dependence.
- **Flow**: state savings on chemical fertiliser subsidy shared with states to promote organics.

## Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH)
- **What**: subsidy on planting material, drip, polyhouse, mulch, protected cultivation, cold chain.
- **Rates**: 40–50% subsidy (small farmers higher).
- **Apply**: state horticulture department.

## National Bee & Honey Mission
- **What**: subsidy on bee boxes, honey extractors, training.
- **Rate**: 75% subsidy for SC/ST/NE; 50% for others.

## FPO Formation (10,000 FPOs scheme)
- **What**: financial support of up to ₹18 lakh + management cost for 5 years + credit guarantee.
- **Benefit for farmer**: pooled bargaining, bulk input purchase, direct market access.
- **Apply**: SFAC / NABARD / NCDC as implementing agencies.

## PMEGP (for rural micro-enterprises)
- **What**: 25–35% subsidy on project cost up to ₹50 lakh (manufacturing), ₹20 lakh (services).
- **Apply**: KVIC portal or DIC.

## State-Specific Highlights
- **Karnataka**: Raita Vidya Nidhi (farmer child scholarship), Krishi Bhagya (farm ponds, 90% subsidy for small farmers), Ksheera Bhagya (dairy incentive).
- **Maharashtra**: Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Kisan Karj Mukti Yojana (loan waiver); Dr. BAMCAI — Vidarbha cotton drip scheme.
- **Punjab**: Happy Seeder subsidy (80%) to avoid stubble burning.
- **Telangana**: Rythu Bandhu — ₹10,000/acre/year input subsidy (was ₹8,000 earlier — check current).
- **Andhra Pradesh**: YSR Rythu Bharosa — ₹13,500/year (Centre ₹6,000 + State ₹7,500) + other components.
- **Odisha**: KALIA — ₹10,000/year for small/marginal.
- **Tamil Nadu**: Uzhavan App, free power to agri pumps.
- **Kerala**: Subhiksha Keralam vegetable scheme; Coconut Development Board schemes.

## How to Actually Apply — Walk-through
1. Check Aadhaar + bank account are linked.
2. Get your land records (khasra/khata/pahani/record of rights).
3. Visit nearest **CSC (Common Service Centre)** — they can file almost all schemes.
4. Alternatively: **agri officer** at block/tehsil office; **KVK** for advisory.
5. Save application number + screenshot; follow up every 30 days.
6. For technical queries: Kisan Call Centre → **1800-180-1551** (toll-free, local language).

## Do NOT Promise Exact Amounts Without Verification
Scheme amounts and eligibility change year-to-year. Always end scheme advice with:
> "Exact current amount and latest eligibility rules — please confirm at your nearest CSC or block agri office before applying."
