# Livestock — Dairy, Poultry, Goat, Fisheries

## Dairy — Most common supplementary income
### Breed Selection
- **Indigenous high-yielders**: Gir, Sahiwal, Red Sindhi, Tharparkar, Rathi, Kankrej — heat-tolerant, disease-resistant, lower maintenance.
- **Crossbred**: HF × Sahiwal, Jersey × Sahiwal — higher yield (12–15 L/day) but need better management + cooler environment.
- **Buffalo**: Murrah (best — 10–15 L), Mehsana, Jaffarabadi.
- Don't chase pure HF in hot plains — heat stress kills yield.

### Feeding (per day for a 350 kg milch cow)
- **Green fodder**: 25–30 kg (berseem/lucerne in winter; maize/sorghum/bajra green in summer).
- **Dry fodder**: 5–8 kg (paddy straw, wheat bhusa, jowar kadbi).
- **Concentrate**: 1 kg per 2 L milk + 1 kg for maintenance. Balanced mix of maize + oil cake + wheat bran + mineral mixture.
- **Mineral mix**: 50 g/day — prevents milk fever, infertility.
- **Clean water**: unlimited access, 40–60 L/day.

### Common Diseases
- **FMD (Foot & Mouth)**: vaccinate every 6 months — FREE under govt programme.
- **HS (Haemorrhagic Septicaemia)** & **BQ (Black Quarter)**: annual vaccination before monsoon.
- **Mastitis**: most common — check udder daily, clean before milking, strip first few squirts to check for clots. Antibiotics under vet advice only.
- **Theileria / Babesia** (tick-borne): tick control via neem wash / cypermethrin pour-on; vaccinate in endemic areas.

### Productivity Tips
- **AI (Artificial Insemination)** via govt vet — sexed semen for female calf preferable.
- **Deworming**: every 3 months (albendazole / fenbendazole).
- **Hoof care**: trim if overgrown; avoid stagnant wet floor.
- **Record-keeping**: milk yield per cow, calving dates, breeding — essential for decisions.

### Economics
- Good dairy animal: ₹70k–₹1.5 lakh purchase.
- Break-even: 2–3 years typical.
- AHDF-KCC gives credit up to ₹3 lakh at effective 4% interest.

## Poultry — Low-capital quick-return
### Broiler (meat)
- **Breed**: Cobb-400, Vencobb, Hubbard.
- **Cycle**: 40–45 days to 2 kg live weight.
- **FCR (Feed Conversion Ratio)**: 1.7–2.0 kg feed per kg gain.
- **Capital**: ₹1–2 lakh for 1000-bird shed; feed is 70% of cost.

### Layer (eggs)
- **Breed**: BV-300, Bovans White.
- **Egg production**: 300+ eggs/bird over 72 weeks.
- **Cycle**: 18 weeks to first egg.

### Backyard (for small farmers)
- **Breed**: Giriraja, Vanaraja, Kadaknath (GI-tagged, premium), CARI Nirbheek.
- **Minimal input**: 50 birds / homestead, ~₹50k annual income.
- Village scheme: NABARD / RKVY backyard poultry subsidies.

### Disease control
- **Ranikhet (ND)**: most deadly — Lasota vaccine at 7 & 28 days + booster.
- **Gumboro (IBD)**: vaccine at 14 & 28 days.
- **Marek's, Fowl Pox**: vaccinate as per schedule.
- **Coccidiosis**: coccidiostat in feed, sanitation.

### Biosecurity
- Lime wash monthly; no outside footwear; foot dip at entry; cull sick birds; don't mix ages.

## Goat / Sheep
### Breeds
- **Milk + meat (goat)**: Sirohi, Beetal, Jamunapari, Barbari.
- **Meat (goat)**: Black Bengal (famous), Osmanabadi, Boer (imported).
- **Sheep**: Deccani, Marwari, Nellore.

### Feeding
- 4–5 hours grazing + 300 g concentrate per adult per day.
- Unlimited water + tree fodder (subabul, gliricidia, hedge lucerne).

### Breeding
- 1 buck per 25 does.
- 2 kiddings in 18 months possible (Black Bengal, Sirohi).

### Diseases
- **PPR (Peste des Petits Ruminants)**: annual vaccine.
- **Enterotoxaemia**: vaccine before monsoon.
- **Foot rot** in monsoon: keep pens dry; zinc sulphate foot bath.
- **Deworm** every 3 months.

### Economics
- A 50-goat unit typically needs ₹2–3 lakh capital.
- 4–6 year ROI.
- Good for landless / small farmers.

## Fisheries
### Composite Fish Culture (pond)
- **Species mix** per 1-acre pond:
  - Catla (surface feeder) — 30%
  - Rohu (column) — 30%
  - Mrigal (bottom) — 20%
  - Common carp — 10%
  - Silver carp — 10%
- **Stocking**: 4000 fingerlings / acre.
- **Feed**: rice bran + oil cake 1:1, @ 2–3% body weight.
- **Harvest**: 10–12 months, 1500–2500 kg/acre.

### Brackishwater Shrimp (coastal)
- **Species**: L. vannamei (fast growth, disease-resistant), Penaeus monodon (native black tiger).
- **Risk**: WSSV (White Spot Syndrome Virus) — use certified SPF seed.
- **Investment**: high (₹5–10 lakh/acre) but high return.

### Schemes
- **PMMSY (Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana)**: ₹20,050 crore — subsidies for ponds, cages, hatcheries, cold chain, boats.
- **Fisheries KCC**: up to ₹2 lakh at effective 4%.

## Apiculture (Beekeeping)
- **Bee species**: Apis mellifera (main commercial), Apis cerana indica (indigenous).
- **Box**: 1 box → 10–20 kg honey/year + pollination service (increases crop yields 15–30%).
- **Capital**: ₹5k per box (bees + box).
- **Scheme**: National Beekeeping & Honey Mission: 75% subsidy for SC/ST/NE, 50% others.
- **Migratory**: follow flowering — mustard (Rajasthan), coriander (Gujarat), eucalyptus (Punjab), litchi (Bihar).

## Sericulture (silk)
### Mulberry silk — Karnataka, TN, AP, WB
- **Capital**: ₹30–50k for 1-acre mulberry + rearing house.
- **Cycle**: 28 days per crop; 5–6 crops/year.
- **Yield**: 60–100 kg cocoons/acre/crop.
- **Income**: ₹2–4 lakh/acre/year for a well-run unit.

### Tasar, Eri, Muga (non-mulberry, tribal / NE)
- Tasar — Jharkhand, Odisha, Chhattisgarh tribal belt.
- Muga (only Assam) — GI-tagged, premium.

## Integrated Farming — Income Stability
- Combine crop + dairy + poultry + fish + beekeeping + kitchen garden — even 1 acre can give ₹2–3 lakh stable annual income vs risky mono-cropping.
- Animal waste → crop fertility; crop residues → animal feed. Circular.
